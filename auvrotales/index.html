How it works

1 Read a short passage aloud. Three scenes, about a minute and a half total.
2 AuvroTales learns the warmth, rhythm, and tone of your voice — on your phone, not in the cloud.
3 Pick a story. Hit play. Your voice narrates every page.
4 Your voice shifts from playful to gentle to sleepy as the story winds down.

Written to a single rule

The energy only goes down. Every story starts warm and winds toward sleep. No villains. No conflict. No fear.
Your child never has to touch the phone. Start playback, turn the screen face-down. The story plays through to the end.
No chrome in the way. Full-screen illustrations, warm light, visible brushstrokes. The art fills the entire screen.

Private by architecture, not by promise

Your voice recording, voice model, and story playback all stay on your iPhone. The raw recording is deleted the moment the voice model is built. The model is encrypted with a hardware-backed key that never leaves your Secure Enclave. No accounts, no sign-ups, no email. We literally don't know who you are, and we can't.

AuvroTales uses the same on-device voice engine that powers SayPaw and VoiceBack. See all our apps at auvro.app.
