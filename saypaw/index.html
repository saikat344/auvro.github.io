How it works

1 Pick a photo from your library or take one.
2 Pick a voice personality.
3 Type what they're thinking. Or tap the dice for a random thought.
4 Tap play. Your pet speaks.
5 Export as video. Post everywhere.

Share-ready videos

Every SayPaw creation exports as a square video (1080x1080) with your pet's photo, animated waveform, and audio — optimized for TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and iMessage. One tap to share.

Completely private

Everything runs on your iPhone. Your pet's photo and voice are processed on-device. Nothing is uploaded. No data is collected. No analytics. We literally don't know you exist, and neither does your pet's therapist.

SayPaw uses the same on-device voice engine that powers VoiceBack and AuvroTales. If you like how it sounds, check them out at auvro.app.
